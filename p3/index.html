who we are

In this band we all have blossoming individual talents and passions. Creativity is everything to us. It’s in the music we make, the videos we create, the art we design, the shows we curate. It is our life-blood and it is what drives us to move forward every day. If you are going through a hard time, I hope our band's journey and everything we do, shines a little light on your life!

We formed this band with our hearts, memories, hopes and dreams, and we want you to listen to every lyric, every melody, and chord. It’s very important to us that we remain diverse in the songs we release and the boundaries that we can break for a modern rock band.

Never did we imagine all those years ago, jamming in a garage in Australia, that we would be releasing our music and performing on stages across the world. This is all for you, 5SOS FAM.

Ashton, Calum, Michael and Luke :-)
